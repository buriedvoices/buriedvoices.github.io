Gameplay Screenshot - Khura in The Library

Buried voices is a 3D puzzle-platformer game set in the fictional world of the Feorlen and Avanqi societies. Playing as a young mixed race woman named Khura, you sneak into the legendary Library, where you discover how the Feorlen have manipulated history to keep the native Avanqi population marginalized—and how far they will go to keep these truths hidden.
